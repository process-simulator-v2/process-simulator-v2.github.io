Process Simulator

Developed by Yuvraj Singh Bawa & Puneet Randar, and Enhanced by Shiva Sai Naidu under the guidance of Dr. Prahald Venkateshan as part of a term project at the Indian Institute of Management, Ahmedabad.

Process Simulator is a pedagogical tool used to provide students of operations management an experiential learning opportunity. Developed with Konva.
